History & Culture

Jeju Island, or Jejudo, is South Korea’s most popular travel destination. It features a stunning volcanic landscape, beach resorts, and scenic hiking trails. The island is also known for its distinct culture and dialect, highlighted by the Haenyeo — free-diving women who embody Jeju’s rich matriarchal traditions.
